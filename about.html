About

I'm Lekehal Hammada, a final-year Industrial Engineering student at ENIT specializing in Data Science for Smart Industry. I'm also pursuing a Master’s degree in the Next Production Revolution (NePRev). Passionate about data analysis, AI, and mathematical modeling, I’ve led applied projects in weather forecasting, stock price prediction, and mental health data analysis. I'm especially interested in how data can drive meaningful impact in fields like energy, healthcare, and logistics.
